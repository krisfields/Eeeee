About This Game

This game was built as a special Christmas present for my 12-year-old son. As any parent knows, communicating with kids can be both delightful and, at times, a bit challenging. Half the time, when I ask him a question, the only response I get is a resounding "Eeeeeee!"

Inspired by these interactions, I wanted to create something that not only entertains but also symbolizes the bond between a parent and child. The idea of the game centers around two halves of yourself that must come together to form a complete whole. It's a metaphor for understanding, patience, and the joy of connecting with those we love most.

I hope this game brings as much joy and connection to others as it did to my family. Merry Christmas and happy gaming!
